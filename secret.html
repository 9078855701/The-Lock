The Other Side

You found it. The person who locked this door
locked it because they had something worth protecting.

What they left behind

Not treasure. Not power. Just a note, folded twice:

"If you are reading this, you were curious enough
to try — and patient enough to figure it out.

That is all anyone can ever ask of another person.

The door was never about keeping people out.
It was about finding the right ones to let in."
